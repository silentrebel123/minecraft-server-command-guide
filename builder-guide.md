### WorldEdit (Survival Server)

`//wand` - Get the WorldEdit wand tool.
`//pos1` - Set position 1 to your current location.
`//pos2` - Set position 2 to your current location.
`//copy` - Copy the selected region.
`//paste` - Paste the copied region.
`//undo` - Undo your last action.
`//redo` - Redo your last undone action.
